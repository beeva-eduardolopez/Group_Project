Our contests are based on real-time point spreads that are used to establish favorites and underdogs.

Instead of picking players based on their statistics, our contests are based on outcomes of games vs. the point spread and the over/under. The concept behind our game is fun, easy, mobile and patent pending.

OVERVIEW OF GAMEPLAY

1

Pick Your Contest

CoverSharks is different because it lets you choose your contest and how you want to play. Enter the Shark Tank and choose college teams to play against the point spread or the total in our weekly games.

2

Choose How You Want to Play

Do you want to compete alongside your favorite teams? Do you follow the entire nation, a specific conference, or region of the country? CoverSharks gives you the option to customize your play with our unique format of games that suits your personal preferences.

Score Points and Win

Once the games begin, teams and players compete to win points. With each win, you accumulate points and move to new and more challenging levels of play.

3

Play for Free

CoverSharks lets you play for free in any of our games. Want to play with more on the line than picking the winner? Choose from a variety of games and choose your competition each and every week of the season.

4

Play with Friends, Family, Rivals, & Co-workers

Once you see for yourself how much fun you will have, play with everyone from friends to enemies for the ultimate game experience. Play as a single player, head-to-head, or choose to join or create a private pool to see who has bragging rights with every game.
